About Royal Kids

A school built on purpose, growing with intention

Established in 2019 and accredited by the Ministry of Education Namibia, Royal Kids Private School offers a structured and inclusive education from Pre-Primary through Grade 7.

Reaching Outwards, Growing Minds, Building Futures

Our story

Royal Kids Private School was established in 2019, offering education from Pre-Primary (Baby Classes) through Grades 1 to 7. As an accredited institution under the Ministry of Education Namibia, we are committed to providing quality education at affordable fees, welcoming both boys and girls in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Our philosophy

At Royal Kids Private School, we promote an education that encourages global citizenship and nurtures futurepreneurs: learners who are conscious of their actions, think critically, and champion dialogue, tolerance, and hope for the future. By fostering interdependence, coexistence, and reconciliation, we help build peaceful communities where people learn and live together.

Our shared vision is to foster a sense of purpose and ignite a passion for lifelong learning within our Royal Kids family. We encourage learners to communicate ideas effectively, work collaboratively, and identify and solve challenges through creative and critical thinking, all while taking action to positively impact our global community.

Reaching Outwards, Growing Minds, Building Futures

Our What, Reaching Outwards

To ignite a passion for lifelong learning within our entire learning community.

Our How, Growing Minds

We encourage learners to communicate ideas effectively, collaborate with others, and tackle challenges using creative and critical thinking, all while making a positive impact on the world around them.

Our Why, Building Futures

To provide our learners with a diverse range of authentic experiences and opportunities, empowering them to find their purpose, make a positive difference, and build their futures.
